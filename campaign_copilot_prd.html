Campaign Copilot — PRD

AI-native Mini CRM · Xeno Engineering Assignment · June 2026

Problem

Marketers at D2C brands know who they want to reach but spend time in forms, segments, and copywriting tools — not thinking about the campaign. Existing CRMs make AI a button. Campaign Copilot makes AI the interface.

The bet

A chat-first experience where the marketer describes intent in natural language. The AI pushes back, asks smart questions, explains its reasoning — and sends the campaign in one click.

Target user

A marketing manager at a fashion or beauty brand. Non-technical. Knows their customers by behavior. Wants to run a campaign in under 5 minutes without learning a tool.

Scope

In scope

Customer + order ingestion (CSV seed) AI-driven segmentation via chat Message drafting via AI WhatsApp + SMS simulation Stubbed channel service with async callbacks Real-time delivery dashboard (SSE) Campaign history

Explicitly out of scope

RCS / Email channels Multi-step campaign sequences Auth / multi-tenant Real messaging providers Manual segment builder UI A/B testing

Core user flow

1
Marketer opens chat
Sees a blank conversational interface. No forms, no nav.
2
States intent
"Re-engage customers who haven't bought in 90 days, offer 10% off"
3
AI responds with segment preview + smart question
"Found 847 customers. Want to exclude VIPs who already got a discount this month?"
4
AI drafts a message
"Here's a WhatsApp message — want it more casual or more urgent?"
5
AI recommends timing
"Tuesday 11am gets 23% higher open rates for this segment."
6
One-click send
Campaign dispatched to stubbed channel service.
7
Live delivery feed
Sent → Delivered → Opened stats update in real time via SSE.
